Internships

Triumph Actuation Systems - Software Engineering

Last summer I returned to Triumph Actuation Systems in Yakima, Washington. At Triumph I designed a natural language processing system for a voice-controlled test computer software platform using the .NET Framework, C#, and Visual Studio. This involved customizing a string comparison algorithm to allow technicians to directly access desired programs and files.

I also wrote two apps to allow the development of instruction books and progress reports and designed a file mirroring and release validation library.


Triumph Actuation Systems - Automation Engineering

Following my first year at UW, I interned at Triumph Actuation Systems, and got a taste of applying technology to the aerospace manufacturing business. Over the summer I independently designed, programmed, troubleshot, and tested microcontroller-based drop counting and volume measuring test fixtures. I also contributed to the design of wireless control software and hardware to communicate between testing computers, tablets, and microcontrollers. Implemented the new wireless technology in production and testing environments. I presented project status updates to the executive management team at three meetings over my 15-week internship.
